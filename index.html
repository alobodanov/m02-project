No Bull Sports is a non-profit organization dedicated to uniting, educating and empowering young female athletes. Our aim is to create a community of female athletes from all walks of life and core to our mission is offering quarterly scholarships to deserving high school students. No Bull Sports strives to provide smart, trailblazing, young women the financial resources they need to achieve their academic and athletic goals.

Our online community will launch on October 11th in honor of International Day of the Girl and we need your support! Help us raise funds for our first scholarship which will be awarded to a deserving young female athlete!
