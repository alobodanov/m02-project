Money Planner is an Android application that was created by Artem Lobodanov (me) in 2016. The purpose of the app is to help people financially for their future plans/goals. Money Planner helps people eliminate all unnecessary spendings in their daily lives to save money. I hope my app will be able to accomplish its purpose and help customers to achieve their financial goals and create a more stable and better future.

About this project

Update: a 3rd size of the Memento will include Pluto, please read the update about it.

After the successful kickstarter campaign for the SpaceTime Coordinates poster & t-shirt, I’m launching my 3rd Kickstarter, a more intimate version of the STC project that you can always carry on you, in case you stumble upon a wormhole or tear in the fabric of space and time.

Use it as a pendant, keyring, push-pin badge or coin for magic tricks. Always have your coordinates at the ready and never get lost in the space–time continuum again!

All Gold products go through extensive hand-polishing to give them a smooth, shiny finish.

14k Gold has a subtle, warm coloring, while 14k Rose Gold has a pinkish tint, 14k White Gold looks like silver with a hint of gold. 18k Gold is brighter and more vibrant than the 14k Gold.

Platinum has a bright silver-white color. It goes through extensive hand-polishing to give it a smooth, shiny finish.

Be sure to check the materials at Shapeways to get a more accurate idea of the colors: shapeways.com/materials/gold & shapeways.com/materials/platinum

Gold and Platinum can be maintained with most non-abrasive cleaners. Avoid exposing to household bleach, which can cause corrosion and discoloration.

Customize your STC Memento!

The Frosted Extreme Detail Plastic can be painted on and you can create a unique personal constellation using a thread and needle.

Manufacturing and Fulfilment

Because each order is customized and printed on demand, please be aware that manufacturing times may change due to high demand. The timeline is only a projection, but we promise to keep you in the loop during the entire fulfilment process.

Delivery

Our partners at Shapeways & i.metarialize will handle most of the shipping, and tracking numbers will be sent to all backers. Please be aware that receiving packages and missed deliveries are the backers’ responsibilities. In the case of reshipment, an additional shipping fee may be added.

Exchange Policy

Exchanges can be made within 30 days through Shapeways and 14 days through i.metarialize in cases of manufacturing mistakes. In the event that something is wrong with your 3D print, please contact Shapeways or i.metarialize ASAP with your order / invoice number.
Please remember to inspect your reward when you receive it, as exchanges cannot be made unless you contact the manufacturer as soon as possible.

Please see our FAQ if you have any additional questions regarding Shipping, Exchange Policy and Customs/Importing.

FAQ

  • Can I pledge for 2 or more of the same reward?

    Yes. Simply multiply the reward pledge (Example: Plastic Memento - $25 x 2 = $50)

    Last updated: Sat, Oct 15 2016 5:11 am EDT
  • Can I get 2 Mementos using 2 different dates?

    Yes, of course you can! :)

    Last updated: Sat, Oct 15 2016 5:11 am EDT
  • Can I pledge for 1 plastic STC and 1 brass STC or any other combination?

    Yes, but please get in touch with us so we can tell you how much to exactly pledge.

    Last updated: Sat, Oct 15 2016 5:11 am EDT
  • Can I pledge for a future date (expecting baby or upcoming wedding)?

    Yes, of course. But keeping track of individual backers over an extended period of time can be difficult. Please make sure to check in with us when your date is decided.

    Also, keep in mind that your collectible card might not be printed if you fail to provide your date before the BackerKit survey deadline. As the minimum quantity at our dear printer Moo is 50 cards, you will receive a digital version of the card instead. A new batch of cards can be printed and sent once the number of backers on hold for a future date exceeds 50.

    Last updated: Sat, Oct 15 2016 5:11 am EDT
  • What diameter chain can be used?

    Any regular thin chain can be easily used with it as the center hole is about ø 1.5 mm. I recommend that you go to your local Jewellery store to find a chain that could go through the center hole. Some chains might require the hook to be removed before passing through the hole. A professional jeweller will be able to do this easily. They can also provide you the option to finely engrave the STC-M (if printed in metal).

    Last updated: Wed, Oct 19 2016 6:59 am EDT
  • Can you engrave the event name on the STC Memento?

    Given the size of the memento and the technology, the results may be unsatisfying. We would recommend going to a Jewellery store and have it engraved the old fashion way.

    Last updated: Sat, Oct 15 2016 5:11 am EDT
  • Will I have to pay customs fee?

    Custom fees might apply to certain materials that are only manufactured in the EU by i.materialise (Raw Steel, Polished Steel, Black Plated Polished Brass & Raw Titanium).

    Shapeways has manufacturers both in the USA & EU, so no custom fee will apply for US & EU backers, for most materials.

    Customs fee might apply to backers outside the USA & EU.

    Last updated: Sat, Oct 15 2016 5:18 am EDT
  • How do I care for the metal printed STC Memento?

    For maintaining your mementos, please refer to Shapeways website for more details: www.shapeways.com/materials

    Last updated: Sat, Oct 15 2016 5:11 am EDT
  • Why don’t you offer deals if we buy 2 or more rewards?

    Each item is unique and printed on demand. Due to the nature of 3D printing, the price per object does not decrease with larger volumes. Because of this, volume price discounts cannot apply.

    Last updated: Sat, Oct 15 2016 5:11 am EDT
  • What if there’s a problem with the reward I receive?

    Shapeways offers a 30 day return policy. Please make sure to get in touch with Shapeways and refer your invoice number if you experience any manufacturing issues.

    i.materialise offers a 14 day return policy. Please make sure to get in touch with them and refer your invoice number if you experience any manufacturing issues.

    Please remember to inspect your reward when you receive it, as exchanges cannot be made unless you contact the manufacturer as soon as possible.

    Last updated: Sat, Oct 15 2016 5:14 am EDT
  • My shipment got returned to you and I didn’t get any delivery notification!

    Once you receive your tracking info (via BackerKit) it is your responsibility to check on the status and get in touch with your local mail carrier in case they fail to leave a notice. In the event your package gets returned to us, please understand that you may be charged for re-shipping.

    Please note that all premium STC-M (Titanium, Solid Gold and Platinum) are sent with signature confirmation delivery.

    Last updated: Tue, Oct 18 2016 6:50 am EDT
  • Exchange Policy

    Exchanges can be made within 30 days through Shapeways and 14 days through i.metarialize in cases of manufacturing mistakes. In the event that something is wrong with your 3D print, please contact Shapeways or i.metarialize ASAP with your order / invoice number.
