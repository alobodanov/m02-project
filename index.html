Money Planner is an Android application that was created by Artem Lobodanov (me) in 2016. The purpose of the app is to help people financially for their future plans/goals. Money Planner helps people eliminate all unnecessary spendings in their daily lives to save money. I hope my app will be able to accomplish its purpose and help customers to achieve their financial goals and create a more stable and better future.

About this project

Sam went to 3 year program for computer science at University. He finishes the program but there is something that is on his mind. Problems:
-He does not have enough money to pay off his tuition loan.
-After graduation, Sam was trying really hard to find a job, but since he always spends money, he could never afford a suit for an interview.
-He moved to a new city where he wanted to find a job. But the cost of rent was way to high for him. So he could never afford a place that he wanted.
About me:
Hi My name is Artem and I am the founder of Money Planner app and I am here to help Sam. I have made an application that will help his budget and to pay off his loan faster then he thinks he can. Simply download the app. Include the money you make and spend. And an app will give you a suggestion what he could do with a load so he will never see it again.

Solution:
-Sam downloads the Money planner app and finds out that it can help him to solve his loan problem.
-He got a job that he wanted and starts to pay off his loan faster then though he will.
-And today he is happy and planing to keep on saving money by using Money Planner to buy a house.

Money App is free to use, will give you a solution to a money problem and it will help to achieve your goals and to have better things in life.
Thank you for stopping by and we are looking forward seeing you and plesae dont forget to follow us on twitter or instagram :)

Money app helps to delivear a better money managment to a user. When a user sets a goal what they want to porchas in the future, an app will help to calculate and display how long will it take to save up a spasific ammount. One of its unique features, is that it can give a suggestion to a user on how to save money for a specific goal.

It is important to have an emergency fund set aside to cover unexpected expenses. This could cover an unexpected car repair, your emergency appendectomy or a sudden job loss. Ideally your emergency fund should be about three to six months of your expenses. If you are just starting out you should put aside at least $1000.00 for this. In addition to your emergency fund you need to make sure you have a plan and good insurance in place to help you survive the unexpected financial events in your life. Once you are out of debt, you should work on bringing your emergency fund up to between six to twelve months of your income. If you are single or living on just one income, you may want to go with a larger emergency fund
